OUR STORY

" Embracing Women's Confidence Through Fashion "
COCOSIN is Vietnamese fashion brand based in Ho Chi Minh since 2012. We offer splendidly trendy clothes for women with affordable price.Our brand was found with a mission to inspire women and to embrace their confidence in fashionable presence.

We dedicate our brand to celebrate the image of a modern woman, who is confident, courageous, daring and fashionable.

COCO SIN makes effort to deliver those elements to each and every customers through authentic design language.

As at the frontier of building a local brand, COCO SIN's dream is to set an example and assert a positive message to other up-rising Vietnamese brands in both international and domestic domain.
